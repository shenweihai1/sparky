Patents

Big data: An architecture for the real-time analysis
Weihai Shen;Junxiu Gao;Qi Tan;
Abstract: With the advent of big data era, the most crucial problem a large number of Internet companies have faced is how to obtain users’ real-time behavior information, which has given rise to big-data real-time analysis. The traditional analysis architecture simply analyze data based on offline data. However, offline data usually cannot guarantee the timeliness of data. In this industrial patent, we design an architecture to provide users with real-time statistic information even with big data.

Academic projects

1. Emotion-Preserved Face Recognition with Privacy Protection
Abstract：Face recognition is becoming an increasingly important topic in the area of computer vision. A typical application of face recognition is to identify and label all of the human faces in a given image or video clip. However, the rapid improvement and widespread deployment of this emerging technology also raise a lot of privacy concerns. In this project, we propose a strongly privacy-enhanced face recognition system, which achieves effective privacy protection, while still preserves the important human emotion information.
